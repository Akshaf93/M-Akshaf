THE CHALLENGE

The rover required high torque to climb 45-degree inclines, but the available motors were high-speed/low-torque. Space within the wheel hub was extremely limited.

THE SOLUTION

Designed a compact planetary gear system that fits inside the wheel rim. The involute gear profiles were modified for 3D printing to reduce friction and backlash.
