§01

Brief

THE CHALLENGE

The rover required high torque to climb 45-degree inclines, but the available motors were high-speed/low-torque. Space within the wheel hub was extremely limited.

THE SOLUTION

Designed a compact planetary gear system that fits inside the wheel rim. The involute gear profiles were modified for 3D printing to reduce friction and backlash.

§02

Gear Profile Optimization

Standard involute gears often bind when 3D printed due to the "elephant foot" effect and general tolerance issues. For this planetary gearbox, we modified the pressure angle to 25 degrees to increase tooth strength and applied a negative offset to the tooth thickness.
