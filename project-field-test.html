Real-world testing and validation of the mechanical systems in harsh environments.

PROJECT DATA

Location

Outdoor Terrain

Duration

2 Weeks

Metrics

Thermal, Stress, Battery Life

THE CHALLENGE

Theoretical designs often fail in the real world due to unforeseen variables like dust ingress, vibration, and thermal throttling. The goal was to push the rover to failure to identify weak points.

THE SOLUTION

Conducted a series of stress tests including drop tests, incline stalls, and continuous runtime tests. Data was logged via telemetry to analyze motor temperatures and battery discharge rates.
